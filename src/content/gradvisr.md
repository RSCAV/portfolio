# gradvisr

**AI degree planner for college students.**

Most college students have no clear picture of what they need to graduate. Degree audits are buried in clunky portals, advisors are overbooked, and the result is that students take wrong classes, miss requirements, or graduate late.

Gradvisr fixes that.

---

## what it does

- Upload your transcript
- Tell it your major, minor, and goals
- It maps out every requirement you've completed and what's left
- Suggests a semester-by-semester plan based on your goals and schedule
- Flags conflicts, prerequisites, and dead ends before you hit them

It knows your university's course catalog. It understands transfer credit. It doesn't replace your advisor — it makes your advising appointments actually productive.

---

## why it matters

Advising at scale doesn't work. There are thousands of students and not enough advisors. Gradvisr is the layer that handles the routine stuff so that human advisors can focus on the edge cases that actually need judgment.
