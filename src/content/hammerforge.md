# hammerforge

**MMA gym in Gainesville, FL.**

Hammerforge needed a website that looked as serious as the training. Clean, bold, functional — schedules, classes, membership info, and a way for new people to get in the door.

---

## the project

Built the full website from scratch. Design and development. They had a logo, a vibe, and a list of what they needed. I handled everything else.

- Schedule and class listings
- Instructor profiles
- Membership tiers and pricing
- Contact and sign-up flow
- Mobile-first (most gym traffic is mobile)

---

## stack

Next.js, Tailwind, Vercel. Simple and fast. No CMS overhead — the gym staff updates content through a clean admin interface.

---

## status

Delivered and live. Ongoing maintenance and updates as needed.
